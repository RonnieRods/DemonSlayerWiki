Quick Answers

What is the significance of Obanai being the first male born into his clan in over 370 years?
The birth of Obanai Iguro, the first male in his clan in 370 years, was significant due to the clan's sacrificial practices to a snake-like demon they worshipped. The clan, living luxuriously off stolen wealth, offered newborns to the demon. Obanai's male birth and heterochromia led to his confinement and cultivation for years, to grow to a size that would satisfy the demon's appetite.
Why was Obanai Iguro confined in his early life?
Born as the first male in his family in 370 years, Obanai Iguro was confined due to his clan's practices. His family, a clan of thieves, worshipped a demon with a snake-like lower body. They sacrificed newborns to this demon in exchange for wealth. Obanai was kept in a wooden cage and overfed with rich food. His heterochromia caught the demon's interest, leading to his prolonged confinement as the demon decided to let him grow before eating him.
What kind of demon did Obanai's clan worship and why?
Tha clan of Obanai Iguro, a key character in the Obanai manga, worshipped a Serpent Demon. This demon, resembling a woman with a snake-like lower body, was fed the clan's offspring in return for wealth from her attacks. Obanai, the first male born in the clan in 370 years, was intended as a sacrifice to this demon, a crucial part of the Obanai backstory.
What is the story behind Obanai's demon slayer mark?
During the climactic battle against Muzan, Obanai Iguro, the Snake Hashira, activated his Demon Slayer Mark. This mark, appearing as three snake-like tattoos with large dots, extended from his left arm to his chest's left side. The Demon Slayer Mark, a mystical symbol awakened under certain conditions, significantly amplified Obanai's physical prowess.
How does Obanai's backstory influence his character in the manga?
Obanai Iguro's character in the manga is shaped by his backstory. His gynophobia stems from his birth into a female-dominated family that used him as a tool. His facial bandages, which he only removes for the final battle, symbolize his traumatic past. Obanai's strict and demanding nature is also a product of his upbringing. Despite his harsh past, he displays remarkable determination, persisting in his fight against Muzan.

History

Obanai became the first male born into the clan in over 370 years and was confined because of this. His clan was full of thieves who lived off the riches that had been stolen from those killed by a demon that they worshipped which took the form of a woman with a snake-like lower body and face, having a great taste for newborn babies and is what the family had sacrificed to her in exchange for their riches.


Obanai Iguro lived out the beginning of his life locked in a wooden cage being excessively spoiled by his family as they insisted on bringing him countless plates of rich food that he even became sick of it due to the overly pungent odors of the food and the poor ventilation of his room. The Serpent Demon also took a liking to Obanai, since he was a rare male child with heterochromia, and would spend the nights slithering around his cage in the dark and staring at him, which frightened Obanai so much that he couldn't sleep until the noises were gone. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Page 10). [1]


When he turned twelve, Obanai was finally allowed to leave his cell for the first time in his entire life. He was taken into a room that appeared to be an expensive shrine, and put face to face with the Serpent Demon for the first time, immediately causing him to shake in fear and terror at the demon's appearance. Trembling at the realization that this was the very same thing slithering in his room staring at him every night of his life, the demon remarked that Obanai was very small and decided she should wait to eat him when he was bigger. She ordered his mouth to be cut wider all the way to his ears to match hers due to her taking a "liking" of him. The blood that he shed from this procedure was then collected into a chalice that she then drank from. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Pages 12-15). [2]


Imprisoned back in his cell with his wound now covered by a bandage, Obanai began only to think of escape and survival upon realizing his fate. He began to scratch away at the lattice of his cell with a stolen hairpin he took from one of his relatives that he would then use during the night in order to prevent anyone from noticing his attempt to escape. Day by day he would be in a constant state of worry and fear, anxious at the possibility of what would happen if the Serpent Demon or his family would discover his attempt at freedom. The only being then that he could trust was a small snake that had somehow strayed into his cage and became his only friend, Kaburamaru. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Page 15). [3]


Eventually, Obanai finally manages to completely remove a wooden chunk of the bars and escaped into the night. As he ran, the Serpent Demon began to pursue him, almost catching up to him and killing him before the Flame Hashira, Shinjuro Rengoku, by chance came to his rescue. Because of Obanai's escape, the Serpent Demon had killed nearly all fifty of his relatives; the only surviving member of his bloodline was his older cousin, who had also been saved by the Hashira. When they were reunited however, she rejected him and began to violently attack and claw at him due to her blaming him for their family's deaths at the hand of the demon in result to their mistakes of allowing him to escape, screaming at him that he should have just let her eat him. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Pages 15-18). [4]


Due to these harsh words from his cousin and his own sense of self loathing and guilt for having been born into such a family and allowing them to die for his survival, Obanai joined the Demon Slayer Corps and decided to turn all his rage onto whatever demons he could find, believing that he would feel like a better person if he risked his life for the sake of others and somehow ease his guilt. Despite this, he constantly felt as if his dead family would continue to drag him down despite the good deeds he accomplished as a Demon Slayer, and his lingering self loathing would haunt him everyday.


After some time, Obanai had encountered someone within the Demon Slayer Corps who was distressed over getting lost; Mitsuri Kanroji. Helping the young woman to find her way around, they became acquainted and quickly became close. Obanai especially enjoyed being with Mitsuri as he felt like he was a normal boy due to her brimming optimism.


Synopsis

Rehabilitation Training Arc

As the other Hashira discuss what to do with Nezuko Kamado, Obanai, lounging in a nearby tree, inquires how they would deal with Giyu Tomioka's breach of the Demon Slayer conduct. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45 (Pages 5-6). [5] When Tanjiro insists Nezuko would never harm humans despite turning into a demon, the Serpent Hashira tells him to not make such reckless remarks, as the girl would obviously protect her family, and as such he cannot trust anything the young Demon Slayer says. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45 (Pages 8-9). [6] Soon afterwards, he notes with interest that even though Giyu had interjected to stop Tanjiro and Sanemi Shinazugawa's brief scuffle, the former had still managed to land a blow on the latter. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45 (Page 19). [7]


Upon Kagaya's arrival, the Serpent Hashira descended the tree and bowed in respect to his superior. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 46 (Page 4). [8] He then reiterated his distrust for Tanjiro and Nezuko when his leader asked for all of them to accept the two, stating that they all hate demons. He subsequently listened as Kagaya's attendant read Sakonji Urokodaki's letter concerning Tanjiro and Nezuko, and how Sakonji, Tanjiro, and Giyu would all slit their stomachs should Nezuko ever attack a human. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 46 (Pages 8-11). [9] When Kagaya revealed that Tanjiro had come into contact with Muzan Kibutsuji, a mess of questions and arguments broke out, but Obanai and the other Hashira are silenced by a simple gesture from their leader. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 46 (Pages 14-16). [10]


After Sanemi purposely cuts himself to bait Nezuko into attacking him, Obanai reminded his fellow Hashira that he would have to get out of the sunlight in order for her to appear. He then swiftly pinned Tanjiro to the ground to prevent him from interfering, Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 47 (Pages 2-4). [11] maintaining his hold despite Shinobu's protests.


Tanjiro continued to struggle heavily and eventually succeeds in breaking his restraints, but before Obanai could do anything further to him, Giyu grabbed his hand, allowing Tanjiro to escape from his grasp. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 47 (Pages 7-10). [12] Obanai angrily removed his hand from Giyu's grip, demanding to know what he is planning. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 47 (Page 14). [13] The Serpent Hashira was then chastised by Kagaya for his treatment of Tanjiro, causing him to reluctantly agree to refrain from such methods in the future. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 47 (Page 18). [14]


Mugen Train Arc

Obanai stands on a rooftop alone after learning of Kyojuro Rengoku's death, staring into the distance as he expresses his disbelief. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 66 (Page 19). [15]


Entertainment District Arc

Following the battle in Yoshiwara against the Upper Six demons Daki and Gyutaro, Obanai arrived to survey the aftermath. He found Tengen Uzui surrounded by his three wives and partially congratulated him on his mission, though he makes emphasis that the demons were still the least of the Upper Rank. Though Tengen ignores his empty praise, Suma chides Obanai for his late arrival, causing Kaburamaru to hiss at her. Seeing his severe wounds, he wonders on when Tengen will be able to continue his role as the Sound Hashira, only for his belief to be shot down when Tengen intends to retire.


Angered, Obanai demands that Tengen fight till his death, citing that the newer Demon Slayers are weak and believing that someone "as uninspiring as him" is still needed. However, to his surprise, the Serpent Hashira is informed of Tanjiro's rising strength and potential to take his position. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 97. [16]


Hashira Training Arc

Obanai was present at the emergency Hashira meeting at the Ubuyashiki Estate. After Sanemi complains about not encountering any Upper Rank himself, Obanai comments that bumping into one is rare and then asks Mitsuri and Muichiro how they're doing. Amane Ubuyashiki arrives and apologizes for the absense of her husband. Obanai and the other Hashira bow to pay their respects. Amane informs the Hashira about the Demon Slayer Marks and asks Mitsuri and Muichiro to tell about how and what the conditions are to acquire one. Obanai is visibly surprised when Amane mentioned that these marked Demon Slayers were close to defeating Muzan. When Mitsuri tries to explain how she got her mark, Obanai expresses embarrassment. After that, Muichiro tells about hsi theory on the conditions to acquire a Demon Slayer Mark to Obanai and the others. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 128. [17] When Giyu comments that he envies those simpletons who think gaining a mark is simple, Obanai glares at him, but says nothing. When the meeting ends, Giyu prepares to leave and tells the remaining Hashira to make future plans to acquire marks by themselves. Obanai angrily retorts that Giyu does not understand his position as the Water Hashira and asks if he plans to get a head start on them. Giyu simply states that he is not like them. The other Hashira protest Giyu's decision as well, but before the situation escalates into fighting, Gyomei slams his hands together making Obanai and all of the others freeze in their tracks. Gyomei states he has a proposition. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 129. [18]


This proposition turns out to be a special training session called "Hashira Training". Obanai is put in charge of teaching proper sword techniques. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 130. [19] When Tanjiro arrives to his training, Obanai states he has been waiting for him. Tanjiro tries to introduce himself, but Obanai tells him to shut up. He tells Tanjiro that he has heard about him from Mitsuri, but warns him that unlike her, he is not a nice person. Obanai reveals that his training consists of swinging your sword while avoiding obstacles. He leads Tanjiro to a room where he has tied up all the students, making them into a sort of obstacle course. When Tanjiro questions Obanai about the students and what crimes have they committed to be tied up, he menacinglg answers that they committed crimes of weakness, forgetting, taking their time and pissing him off. For the next four days, Obanai trains Tanjiro to hit him while not hitting any of the tied up students. During the fourth day, Tanjiro finally manages to slice off the haori Obanai was wearing, thus completing the training. Afterwards, Obanai impatiently demands Tanjiro to leave immediately and to never talk to Mitsuri again. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 132. [20]


Obanai rushes to the Ubuyashiki estate after hearing the call that Muzan was there. However, Kagaya blows up the estate before he or the other Demon Slayers can arrive, killing himself, Amane Ubuyashiki, Nichika Ubuyashiki and Hinaki Ubuyashiki while heavily wounding Muzan. Gyomei Himejima is the only Demon Slayer to know of this plan beforehand, as Kagaya knew the other Hashira would oppose his decision. After seeing Muzan, Obanai readies himself to unleash his first form on him, but Muzan transports him and the rest of the Hashira into his Infinity Castle. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 139. [21]




Final Battle Arc


Infinity Castle Arc

Obanai plummets into the Infinity Castle before landing onto a platform with Mitsuri, killing a group of Demons with the Serpent Breathing, Fifth Form: Slithering Serpent. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 140. [22]


Later, he and Mitsuri encounter Nakime. Mitsuri jumps off a wall and lunges at the Demon, but Nakime uses her Blood Demon Art to erect a wall and send Mitsuri falling. Obanai jumps in and grabs her, getting her safely to another of the Infinity Castle's pillars. As Nakime starts manipulating the Infinity Castle in the hope of crushing the pair, Obanai is easily able to dodge. After Mitsuri tries to attack and was sent tumbling out a window, Obanai tries to use the Serpent Breathing, Second Form: Venom Fangs of the Narrow Head to decapitate the Demon. However, he too is sent out a window, expressing in a frustrated fashion how much of a pain Nakime's Blood Demon Art is to deal with, despite it not being very deadly. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 164. [23]


After fighting Nakime for a while, Obanai and Mitsuri are informed of Muzan's revival and receive orders to group up with the other Hashira. After hearing how Muichiro and Genya sacrificed their own lives in order to defeat Upper One, Obanai berates himself for not contributing as much to the battle as others have. He remarks how Nakime's Blood Demon Art makes it impossible for him to settle the fight. He thinks he must somehow find a way to get out of this situation. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 181. [24]


Sunrise Countdown Arc

With the help of Yushiro and his Blood Demon Art: Blindfold, Obanai and Mitsuri are able are able to get away from Nakime and they arrive to assist Giyu and Tanjiro who were fighting against Muzan. Obanai appears by Tanjiro's side, who was almost killed by Muzan, and tells him that he is a burden that needs to fallback. Tanjiro ignores Obanai's advice and instead says he is glad that Obanai is still alive. Obanai tells him to worry about himself first instead of others. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 182. [25]


As Muzan grows furious over Nakime's failure, the battle between the Demon Slayers and Muzan resumes. Obanai uses his first form to attack Muzan, who blocks the attack. As the Infinity Castle starts to rapidly change due to Yushiro manipulating Nakime, the floor below the Demon Slayers and Muzan disappears. Obanai falls and lands on his feet on the platform below. Muzan continues his attacks which Obanai avoids. Obanai worryingly thinks that they need to get out of the Infinity Castle before it crumbles, or they will all die. As Mitsuri is caught off guard by the platform rising, Muzan uses this chance to attack her, but Obanai jumps in to get her out of the way of Muzan's attack thus saving her life. The Infinity Castle crumbles, but due to Yushiro, Obanai and the others are safely transported to the surface. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 183. [26]


As the battle moves on to the surface, Obanai emerges from the wreckage of the Infinity Castle. A crow informs the combatants that an hour and a half remain until morning. Muzan blasts the wreckage aside with his newly manifested Spine Whips and taunts the Hashira to try and keep him there for that long. Muzan starts attacking once again. Obanai uses his third form and manages to get close to Muzan. He starts cutting off his head and remarks that even if Muzan does not die from getting his head cut off at least they could weaken him by slicing him to pieces. This does not prove to be the correct move as Obanai is shocked when his attack does nothing to Muzan. Obanai and the others realize that Muzan is able to instantly regenerate to the point that it is useless to try to weaken him using this tactic. As they figure out their mistakes, they also realize they got too close to Muzan and are about to be killed by him. However, before that can happen, Obanai and the others are shielded by lower ranking Demon Slayers who use their own bodies as shields. Obanai is grabbed by one whose entire lower half is sliced off. He is visibly shocked by this sacrifice but says nothing. Muzan reveals that just getting scratched by him once is a death sentence as he points to Tanjiro who is laying down on the ground lifeless. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 184. [27]


Obanai and the two other Hashira continue their fight against Muzan. Obanai remarks on how he can't continuously breathe even with the three Hashira there due to Muzan's speed. As the battle rages on, Obanai is slashed once in the head and once in the shoulder by Muzan, but he continues to fight. Mitsuri, however, is immediately affected by Muzan's attack and falls over. Obanai yells her name, but she tells Obanai and Giyu to protect themselves instead. Before she is killed by Muzan, Gyomei appears and deflects the attack with his flail. At the same time, Sanemi appears and uses the opportunity to slice Muzan in half and sets him on fire. The battle continues with Obanai and the four other Hashira joining forces. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 185. [28]


As the battle continues and the Hashira keep getting pressed on, Obanai thinks about how he can't even shield Gyomei from attacks anymore and soon he will be unable to move himself. When Mitsuri is hit by another attack which forces her to the ground, Obanai is worried and hurries to her side. As Muzan prepares to slice both of them, the three other Hashira shield them while Obanai carries Mitsuri to safety. He entrusts her to a Kakushi and tells them to find Yushiro. Mitsuri tries to argue that she can still fight, but Obanai tells her she has done enough. He tells the Kakushi to take care of her and immediately rushes back towards the battle, ignoreing Mitsuri's pleas. As he runs, he starts to ponder about how many people would still be alive if demons didn't exist. He wonders how things would have been if he and Mitsuri had met under more peaceful circumstances. However, he dismisses this thought and considers it impossible. He thinks back on his childhood and how he wants to die defeating Muzan in order to get rid of his family's impure blood. Obanai hopes that when he dies and if he and Mitsuri both reincarnate into a world without Demons, he can tell Mitsuri that he loves her. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188. [29]


After joining the battle once again, Obanai manages to cut off Muzan's arm by kicking and slashing the arm at the same time. Muzan notices that it is hindering his instant regeneration but remains unworried as it only buys them a miniscule amount of time. Obanai thinks to himself that Muzan's arms have mouths that have a dangerous inhalation ability. In order to avoid it, he has to make huge movements that take up a lot of stamina. He proclaims to himself that they cannot afford to lose as he does not want all of the sacrifices thus far to be in vain. As the battle progresses, Giyu loses his grip on his sword, but Obanai shields him from Muzan's attack and at the same time, Sanemi tosses a new sword to Giyu. After 16 minutes of fighting since coming to the surface, Muzan is surprised by how long the Hashira have managed to last. As Muzan's poison is starting to affect the Hashira more and more, Obanai realizes that the poison is affecting him more than others due to his small body. In that moment, Chachamaru appears carrying antidotes to Muzan's poison. Obanai and the Hashira receive the antidotes, but Chachamaru is killed in the process. As Muzan grows furious, Obanai considers that he needs to find a mroe effective way to attack. He believes that he has contributed the least out of everyone. Thinking back on the report that Muichiro had turned his blade red during his final moments, he speculates that by gripping the sword as hard as you can, you can increase the temperature of the blade and thus turn it crimson red. In that moment, his Demon Slayer Mark appears, and he grips his sword turning it bright red. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 189. [30]


After turning his blade red, Obanai's first thought was that he had made a mistake. He believes he was too eager to turn his blade red and used too much strength to do it which almost made him faint due to him being deprived of oxygen. Sanemi yells Obanai's name as Muzan sends an attack towards Obanai. In that moment, Obanai is unable to do anything, but to think of the fact that if even on of them falls they will no longer be able to break Muzan's attacks. In the last moment before the attack hits Obanai, he's thrown into the air by an invisible Inosuke. Muzan realizes three new opponents have joined the fight, Inosuke, Zenitsu and Kanao, but they are using Yushiro's Blood Demon Art to render themselves invisible. As Muzan was figuring out what was happening, Obanai landed back on the ground. Using the trio's distraction to his advantage, he slices Muzan's hand off with the Serpent Breathing, Third Form: Coil Choke. The sliced body part regenerates much slower which inspires the combatants to continue fighting even harder. Obanai once again attacks and uses his third form against Muzan. This allows the other Hashira to get a chance to turn their weapons red in their own respective ways. A crow informs that one hour and three minutes remain until dawn, meaning the battle has raged on between the Hashira and Muzan for 27 minutes since they came to the surface. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 190. [31]


Obanai, the other Hashira and the trio continue pressing on Muzan scoring several hits. Gyomei accesses the Transparent World and finds out Muzan has multiple hearts and brains. Gyomei yells to Obanai to focus on Muzan's body and tell him if he can see through it. Obanai tries it and sees through it for an instant which confuses him. In that moment, Muzan unleashes his leg whips, which catch Obanai and the others off guard. Obanai is hit and sent flying. He hits a wall and is rendered unconscious and bleeding. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 191. [32]


Obanai quickly gets up and saves Tanjiro, who was fighting Muzan by himself, from death. Obanai, having grabbed Tanjiro, swiftly dodges Muzan's attacks. Tanjiro is glad to see him, but is quickly horrified when he realizes Obanai's face is mutilated. Obanai explains that the earlier attack by Muzan's leg whips was the one that injured him. He tells Tanjiro to stop worrying about him, because his right eye was always bad and he could barely see out of it and because of that, he instead relies on his snake Kaburamaru to see. After confirming Tanjiro had recovered, he lets go of him. Muzan, who had given chase to them, attacks Obanai, but he is able to dodge and block all of the attacks thanks to Kaburamaru. Tanjiro and Obanai combine forces to attack Muzan both using their respective techniques. While Obanai and Tanjiro continue to dodge and attack, Muzan's body suddenly changes revealing old scars. Tanjiro realizes these must be from Muzan's battle against Yoriichi and may potentially be his visible shown weakpoints. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 194. [33]
