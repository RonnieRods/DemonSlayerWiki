Obanai Iguro (伊い黒ぐろ 小お芭ば内ない Iguro Obanai) is a major supporting character of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer of the Demon Slayer Corps and the Serpent Hashira (蛇へび柱ばしら Koi Bashira) of the Taisho era. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45 (Page 6). [3]

Appearance

Obanai is a youg man of short stature, pale skin complextion, and a lean, muscular build. He has straight-edged raven-black hair of varying lengths, the longest reaching down to his shoulders and the shortest stopping at his cheekbones, which he wears down with two shorter strands hanging between his eyes. His eyes are almond-shaped and tilted upwards on the far sides, and are unique colored due to Obanai possessing heterochromia-his right eye is yellow with a black pupil, and his left eye being turquoise. He is partially blind, barely able to see out of right eye. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 194 (Page 11). [4]


Obanai's mouth is usually hidden with white bandages over his lower face to hide his large facial scars, which were the result of his family horrifically cutting open his mouth to resemble that of a snake's gape. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Page 10). [5]


Obanai wears the standard Demon Slayer Corps uniform, which is black in the manga and tinted a deep koamaru color in the anime. The uniform consists of a button-up jacket with a stand-up collar and long sleeves, adorned with gold buttons, a shirt pocket on the left breast, and the kanji for Destroy (滅めつ Metsu) engraved on the back of the uniform. Beneath the jacket, he wears a white long-sleeved collared undershirt. The uniform is fastened at the waist with a white belt and paired with traditional tattsuke-hakama pants in the same koamaru hue. Over his uniform, Obanai wears his signature black-and-white vertically striped haori, with the hem and cuffs striped horizontally rather than vertically, covering his hands. For legwear, instead of the standard kyahan, Obanai wraps his lower legs in white bandages, giving the visual impression of traditional kyahan wraps. He also wears another set of white bandages around his lower face. His footwear consists of white zōri sandals with purple-blue straps, worn over koamaru-colored tabi socks to match the color tint of his uniform. He is also always seen with his white snake, Kaburamaru, wrapped around his shoulders.


During his battle with Muzan, Obanai and the other remaining Hashira are attacked by the demon's flailing whips, and Obanai is left with three long slanted scratches over his eyes, rendering him completely blind. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 194 (Page 10). [6]


When he was a child, Obanai had waist-length hair that he wore tied down his back with a long white bandage and a regular-looking mouth before it was cut by his family to resemble the Serpent Demon worshipped by his clan. He also wore a plain white kimono.

Personality

Obanai is a very harsh and strict individual who shows no concern for those who do not abide by the rules of the Demon Slayer Corps. His expectations for his fellow Demon Slayers are quite unrealistic, as he was seen berating Tengen Uzui for suffering heavy wounds against the lowest Upper Rank whom he called the "weakest" and telling him to "fight to the death" after Tengen insists on retiring. Tengen's retirement also highlights Obanai's devotion to the future of the Demon Slayer Corps. He does this often at others' expense such as when he tied up lower-ranked Demon Slayers and used them as obstacles during Hashira Training - all for relatively petty reasons. He also sees the newer Demon Slayer recruits as weak and incapable of inprovement, expressing extreme surprise when Tengen informs him that Tanjiro Kamado survived the battle with Daki and Gyutaro. Obanai has a rather prominent habit of pointing at people with his finger in an accusatory manner while berating or talking unfavorably about someone.


Despite his merciless personality, there are two people he cares deeply about: Kagaya Ubuyashiki and Mitsuri Kanroji. He has a crush on Mitsuri, and he often lets his feelings shine through by sharing meals with her and giving her gifts. Obanai seems to let his overprotectiveness of Mitsuri show at times, such as when he threatens Tanjiro for growing too close to her. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 132 (Page 12). [7] It was implied that he has a crush on Mitsuri because of the cheerful personality she retained despite going through very hellish training. Talking with her also made him forget all the strife and conflict he had encountered during his past, making him feel as if he were an ordinary young man. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 200. [8] Like all the Hashira, Obanai has a great deal of respect for Kagaya.


Obanai is noted to possess an immense sense of self-loathing due to the fact that he was born to a selfish and immoral clan who would mercilessly sacrifice even newborns to a demon for their own monetary gain. Obanai considers himself and his family so tainted, that years after the other members were killed, he still felt as if his family were digging their nails into his skin to make sure he couldn't go anywhere. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Page 19). [9] He even goes as far as to believe that, in order for him to ever be worthy to Mitsuri, he would first have to die and cleanse himself of his corrupted blood before ever confessing his feelings to her. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 188 (Page 19). [9]


His upbringing also seems to be the cause of his gynophobia since he was born into a family of only women who treated him like a tool. The trauma of his past can also be seen in the form of his facial bandages that he dons to cover the terrible scars he received, never removing it until the final battle. In addition to the horrible treatment he received from his family, of being treated as a mere tool and sacrifice, Obanai was deeply affected by the words of resentment and hatred from his only surviving cousin when she said that the entire family died simply because he ran away. Although he recognized that her grudge and words were illogical, he chose to accept that he was at fault and even considered himself as corrupt as his family was, believing he was not worthy to live a normal life. This shows that despite the various unpleasant traits of his personality, Obanai actually has a conscience and morals, unlike his family.


Obanai has an incredible sense of determination, continuing the fight against Muzan even after is completely blinded and covered in wounds. His ability to protect Giyu and Tanjiro throughout the fight, as well as thanking Tanjiro for helping him and later even took a blow for him proves that Obanai was able to drop his initial grudges and become more open with people he was rude to in the past.


Abilities

Overall Abilities

As a Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, Obanai is a very powerful and skilled combatant. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45 (Page 2). [10] It was revealed that he was more or less blind in his right eye, meaning he ascended to the highest rank in the Demon Slayer Corps with only a single functioning eye. This, in addition to his small physique and inferior physical strength when compared to his peers, made it especially impressive that Obanai managed to ascend to the title of Hashira. During the events in the Infinity Castle, Obanai was able to quickly decapitate numerous demons on par with Lower Ranks. When engaging in battle with the Demon King, Muzan Kibutsuji, Obanai was able to land a great deal of hits on him, slashing off limbs and landing strikes on his neck over the course of the battle.


After Obanai, Giyu, Sanemi, Gyomei, Zenitsu, Inosuke and Kanao were heavily injured by Muzan's attack, Obanai was the first to regain consciousness and aid Tanjiro despite having his eyes clawed out earlier. While it should be noted that Muzan was severely weakened by the drugs in his body, he and Tanjiro nonetheless managed to fend off and push the Demon King into a corner by themselves, displaying incredible endurance. After getting directly hit by Muzan's Shockwave Energy Blast technique, he still took a hit for Tanjiro when Muzan was about to bite his head off. When the Demon King swelled his flesh and exponentially grew in size, Obanai still possessed enough stamina to unleash one technique that aided in slowing Muzan down. Even thought Obanai lost his life at the end of the battle, he was undoubtedly among the bigger contributors in subduing the Demon King.


Kaburamaru (鏑かぶら丸まる Kaburamaru): Obanai, having always been partially blind in his right eye, developed a combat technique utilizing his snake Kaburamaru. Kaburamaru possesses a unique ability which allows him to read and predict a target's attacks and then relay that information to Obanai. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 194 (Page 15). [11] The full extent of this technique is demonstrated when Obanai is completely blinded by Muzan, but he is still able to continue fighting effectively. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 194. [12] Kaburamaru also seems to be capable of understanding human speech and emotions, as he understood Tanjiro's instructions to grab Yushiro's paper talisman and later shed tears when watching Obanai and Mitsuri succumb to their wounds. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 195 (Page 16). [13] Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 200 (Pages 14-15). [14] Kanao herself stated that Kaburamaru was exceptionally clever. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 204 (Page 22). [15]


Demon Slayer Abilities

Demon Slayer Mark: During the the final battle against Muzan, Obanai awakened his own Demon Slayer Mark, which took the form of three, lavender, slithering snake-like tattoo patterns, stretching from his left arm up to the side of his left shoulder, with two large dots on his biceps and forearm. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 189 (Page 19). [16] The Mark greatly enhanced all of Obanai's physical abilities, as seen where he was able to land powerful sword slashes against Muzan, swiftly dodge and maneuver himself to avoid all of his immensely fast attacks, and increase his physical stamina and durability, allowing him to fight despite being severely injured.

  • Bright Red Nichrin Sword: After remembering how Muichiro Tokito turned his blade red, Obanai puts all of his strength into gripping his blade, turning it bright red. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 189. [17] However, he put too much strength into his grip and almost fainted due to the lack of oxygen. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 190 (Page 2). [18] Turning his Nichrin Sword bright red proved to be extremely effective against Muzan, allowing his attacks to hamper his immediate regeneration and giving the other Demon Slayers a chance to turn their blades bright red as well to increase their offensive output.
  • Transparent World: Though briefly, Obanai managed to access the Transparent World and see Muzan's twelve vital organs (seven hearts and five brains) for a split-second after Gyomei instructed everyone to attempt to do so. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 191 (Page 11). [19]


Physical Abilities

Enhanced Strength: Despite his short stature and lesser physical strength when compared to the other Hashira, Obanai still possesses a significant amount of muscle power, as shown when he pins Tanjiro to the ground to the point that he couldn't move or breathe. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 47 (Page 4). [20] During Hashira Training, he was seen being able to parry and attack Sanemi during their sparring session, causing them both to surmise that they were seemingly neck and neck. Kimetsu no Yaiba Anime: Episode 59. [21] He was also able to slash the body of Muzan Kibutsuji on a multitude of occasions. Obanai was also one of the only Demon Slayers able to turn his Nichirin Sword bright red through sheer grip strength, the other being Muichiro Tokito.


Immense Speed & Reflexes: As a Hashira, Obanai possesses superhuman levels of speed and reflexes. When Tanjiro was about to get hit by what would likely have been a fatal strike from Muzan, Obanai managed to jump in and save him in time. He was capable of keeping up with Muzan Kibutsuji's whip attacks for an extended period of time without the boost granted by the Demon Slayer Mark. After awakening the mark, Obanai was fast enough to slice off both of Muzan's limbs. Even when completely blinded, Obanai was able to evade many of Muzan's attacks, pushing him into a corner, catching up to him and piercing his body while the Demon King was fleeing. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 195 (Page 12). [22]


Immense Stamina & Endurance: Obanai has displayed his phenomenal stamina and endurance on multiple occasions during the events within the Infinity Castle and final battle. Obanai, along with Mitsuri, were able to play an exhausting game of cat and mouse with Upper Rank Four, Nakime for an extended period of time, retaining still enough energy to arrive to Tanjiro's and Giyu's aid. At the surface, Obanai and his allies were able to stall Muzan for around half an hour, sustaining injuries from poison and his flesh whips before the Demon King overpowered them with a devastating attack.


After Muzan incapacitated him, Giyu, Sanemi, Gyomei, Zenitsu, Inosuke and Kanao, Obanai was the first to recover and aid Tanjiro, doing so before all his other fellow Hashira could regather themselves and even after sustaining severe facial wounds that left him blind. Afterwards, Obanai, with Tanjiro's aid, managed to drive Muzan into a corner and fend him off by themselves for another 35 minutes before being hit by Muzan's Shockwave Energy Blast technique which greatly damaged his nervous system. Despite this, Obanai remained steadfast and took a blow for Tanjiro when Muzan was poised to bite his head off. Nearing the end of the battle, Obanai still had enough energy to muster a technique; as such, out of all the Hashira that fought in that battle, Obanai lasted arguably the longest.


Fighting Style

Master Swordsman: As a Hashira of the Demon Slayer Corps, Obanai was one of the most powerful and skilled swordsmen in the entire organization. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 45. [23] This was shown during Hashira Training where he was entrusted to teach all the other Demon Slayers about sword wielding techniques. Obanai makes use of a unique swordsmanship style that utilizes slithering motions to twist and bend his sword and attack in unorthodox ways, allowing him to weave through narrow openings with ease. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 132 (Pages 9-10). [24] His swordsmanship prowess allowed him to slaughter hordes of demons with ease and to keep up with the Demon King himself, Muzan Kibutsuji.


Breathing Style

Serpent Breathing (蛇へびの呼こ吸きゅう Hebi no kokyū): A Breathing Style Obanai created and mastered. This swordsmanship style makes use of twists and turns similar to how a serpent slithers to attack in strange ways. Obanai also learnt to incorporate his pet snake, Kaburamaru (鏑かぶら丸まる Kaburamaru), into this Breathing Style, extending his physical abilities as seen in the final battle, where he could keep up with a weakened Muzan while completely blind.


Techniques

  • First Form: Winding Serpent Slash (壱いちノ型かた   委い蛇だ斬ぎり Ichi no kata: Idagiri) Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 183 (Pages 9-10). [25] - The user moves like a slithering snake and releases several slashes in a winding pattern.
  • Second Form: Venom Fangs of the Narrow Head (弐にノ型かた   狭きょう頭ずの毒どく牙が Ni no kata: Kyōzu no Dokuga) Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 164 (Page 15). [26] - The user dashes behind their opponent at blinding speed and aims to swiftly slice off their head.
  • Third Form: Coil Choke (参さんノ型かた   塒とぐろ締じめ San no kata: Toguro Jime) Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga and Anime: Chapter 190 (Page 14) and Episode 56. [27] - The user circles around their opponent while using their sword to slice them from all directions.
  • Fourth Form: Twin-Headed Reptile (肆しノ型かた   頸けい蛇じゃ双そう生せい Shi no kata: Keija Sōsei) Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 199 (Page 17). [28] - The user leaps forward and performs a horizontal slash that slices through the target.
  • Fifth Form: Slithering Serpent (伍ごノ型かた   蜿えん蜿えん長ちょう蛇だ Go no kata: En'en Chōda) Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga and Anime: Chapter 140 (Page 11) and Episode 56. [29] - The user curves their sword in multiple directions and slices their opponent, making it capable of decapitating multiple enemies at once.

Game Exclusive Techniques

  • Third Form: Coil Choke (参さんノ型かた   塒とぐろ締じめ・威い San no kata: Toguro Jime - I) Kimetsu no Yaiba Game: The Hinokami Chronicles 2. [30] - A slightly extended variant of Coil Choke. The user knocks back their opponent with their sword, before immediately pursuing and appearing behind them. The user delivers a slash from behind and transitions into using Coil Choke, delivering multiple slashes from all different angles, lifting their opponent upwards. The user then appears to create a constriction-like attack while mid-air before diving downwards and delivering a powerful vertical slash from above, causing a massive crater on the ground as they ground their opponent.
  • Fifth Form: Slithering Serpent, Carnage (伍ごノ型かた   蜿えん蜿えん長ちょう蛇だ・破は Go no kata: En'en Chōda - Ha) Kimetsu no Yaiba Game: The Hinokami Chronicles 2. [30] - The user knocks their opponent back using their sword and charges at them while executing a variant of Slithering Serpent, which is so powerful that it takes the form of a snake predating on the opponent. As the user keeps relentlessly slashing them while "inside" the snake, it then coils around into a spiral, before the user delivers a single, horizontal slash as they exit out "the head" while the opponent behind them is constricted in its "coils" before falling to the ground.
  • Leaping Viperstrike (跳ちょう躍やく斬ぎり Chōyaku Giri) Kimetsu no Yaiba Game: The Hinokami Chronicles 2. [30] - The user leaps in the air and attacks the opponent in a twisting and winding slash, and then spirals down on their opponent.
  • Sinuous Slash (蛇だ行こう斬ぎり Dakō Giri) Kimetsu no Yaiba Game: The Hinokami Chronicles 2. [30] - The user delivers several jagged and twisted slashes, before finishing with a backhand slash of equal distortion.
  • United Front: Wind Serpent (風ふう蛇じゃ一いっ体たい Fūja Ittai) Kimetsu no Yaiba Game: The Hinokami Chronicles 2. [30] - A collaboration between a Wind Breathing user and a Serpent Breathing user. The Serpent Breathing user rushes towards their opponent and delivers several bending and winding cuts, before the Wind Breathing user rushes in cyclone-like motion. After attacking once, the Wind Breather turns around and creates an attack remarkably similar to Rising Dust Storm. As the opponent is spinning around the tornado-like attack, the Serpent Breather then enters and slashes them with such a powerful winding momentum that takes the form of a snake predating upon them. As the Serpent Breather pushes their opponent downwards, the Wind Breather joins in to double the force in a violent tornado combining both their Breathing Styles.

Equipment

Twisted Nichirin Katana: Obanai carries around a heavily modified Nichirin katana similar in appearance to an Indonesian Kris, with the blade's shape modeled into a wavy blade that heavily resembles a slithering snake. It is lavender in color, with white outline. It also has a purple wavy pattern in the middle of the blade along with a white, snake-like line. His katana's tsuba has a gold circular shape engraved with two snakes connected by several plant-like patterns, and a purple boarder. His handle is lavender and decorated with a twisting wrap of purple cord, and a gold kashira. As a Hashira, the words Destroyer of Demons (惡あっ鬼き滅めっ殺さつ Akki Messatsu) are engraved on the side of the blade. Obanai also carries along a special scabbard made of leather held together by a weak magnetic pull that opens up whenever he uses his katana, with the scabbard being in white color. It is designed so that Obanai unsheathes his sword vertically instead of horizontally. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Volume 22, (Extra Pages). [31]
