Kanata Ubuyashiki


Kanata Ubuyashiki (産うぶ屋や敷しき かなた Ubuyashiki Kanata) is a major supporting character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She is the youngest daughter of Amane and Kagaya Ubuyashiki. She currently serves her older brother, the current head of the Ubuyashiki Household, Kiriya Ubuyashiki.

Appearance

Kanata is a girl with pale, light skin has a set of large, purple eyes. Her hair is shaped into a blunt white bob haircut similar to the rest of her siblings. She adorns an elongated wisteria headpiece on the right side of her head as opposed to the left side like her brother.


During Final Selection, Kanata wore a purple, flower-patterned Hōmongi kimono with a dark indigo juban under it. She also sported a sheen green obi with a dark indigo obiage and a plum-red obijime tying it.


During the events in the Infinity Castle, Kanata wore a purple Edo komon kimono with a white juban under it, sporting a black obi with a white obiage and a pink obijime, completely identical to her twin sister Kuina Ubuyashiki.

Personality

Kanata's first appearance during the Final Selection depicts herself to be a professional, intelligent, and focused individual on her given task of explaining the proper details regarding the exam. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 8 (Pages 10-11). [3]


Kanata also displays a degree of emotional aloofness when describing the fatal consequences of the exam, given the likely chances that not even half of the gathered trainees would survive the test. This indifferent attitude is also seen during the exams ending when Kanata doesn't display any form of panic, fear, or intimidation despite being manhandled and threatened by Genya Shinazugawa. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 8 (Pages 13-16). [4]


However, Kanata does show a fragile side after the death of her parents and older sisters, breaking into tears when remembering them. Nonetheless, she is able to regain her composure when Kiriya reminds her to concentrate at the task at hand. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 147 (Page 2). [5] Kanata's more emotional side would resurface several times during the Final Battle however, from crying after Kiriya himself is told to compose himself by Kuina, to tearfully praying for Muzan's defeat when he was doing anything in his power to flee.


History

Kanata was born to Kagaya Ubuyashiki and Amane Ubuyashiki as their youngest-born daughter of their five children. Her quintuplet siblings are Hinaki, Nichika, Kiriya, and Kuina.


Kanata was present at Sanemi Shinazugawa's first Hashira meeting, witnessing him accuse Kagaya of being an aloof leader before he reassured the emotionally volatile Wind Hashira. She helped Kagaya walk towards Sanemi so he could deliver Masachika Kumeno's will.


Synopsis

Final Selection Arc

Kanata appears at the Final Selection along with her brother Kiriya and explains to the recruits what awaits them at the selection.


She later appears at the end of the Selection, congratulating all the survivors, and helps her brother explain how the Demon Slayer organization works. She is later hit and grabbed by Genya Shinazugawa by the hair, due to him impatiently wanting a Nichirin Sword immediately. He is stopped by Tanjiro Kamado, although Kanata showed little reaction to the assault or Tanjiro's retaliation.


Entertainment District Arc

Shortly after the battle in Yoshiwara ended with the deaths of Upper Rank demons Gyutaro and Daki, Kanata's father Kagaya became ecstatic at the news. However, his celebration of the new turn of events was short-lived as his health began to deteriorate quickly, coughing blood as Amane rushed for her children to fetch his medication. Kanata was summoned to help Kagaya, worriedly calling to her father as she watched him coughing more and more.


Swordsmith Village Arc

Kanata is indirectly mentioned by Tanjiro when after Genya bitterly reminded him that he broke his arm, Tanjiro proclaimed that he deserved it for hitting Kanata. However, before or after leaving the Swordsmith Village, Genya, now more emotionally mature and respectful, personally apologized to Kanata who accepted.


Hashira Training Arc

Kanata appears alongside her mother and brother during the Hashira meeting, the former taking the place of her bedridden father. She sat by Amane as she explained the history of the Demon Slayer Marks and stared in bewilderment after Mitsuri's nonsensical testimony on how she awakened her's.


Sometime later, her parents devised a plan to set up an ambush for Muzan Kibutsuji, which involved blowing up their mansion with them in it. Kanata's two elder sisters: Nichika and Hinaki chose to stay and die with their parents, while she and Kuina were set to assist their brother Kiriya, who would finally remove his girl's clothing and assume the position of Oyakata. They would be relocated to the hidden Butterfly Mansion, with retired Hashira Tengen Uzui and Shinjuro Rengoku serving as their bodyguards, a first time in generations for the family.


Infinity Castle Arc

Using talisman created by the allied demon Yushiro, Kanata and Kuina help Kiriya map the Infinity Castle; she sat to the right of him, informing them that Zenitsu Agatsuma had run into the new Upper Rank Six. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 144 (Pages 5-8). [6] Later on while mapping the Infinity Castle, Kanata became overwhelmed by the deaths of her parents and sisters and begun to tear up. She is scolded by Kiriya, who tells her not to stop her hand, to which she complied. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 147 (Page 1). [7]


During the battle against Upper Rank One Kokushibo, Sanemi remembered Kanata's presence at his first Hashira meeting. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 168 (Pages 8-15). [8] In addition, a crow surveilled the battle and the siblings watched it via Yushiro's Blood Demon Art. Kuina asks Kiriya if they should send Tanjiro and Giyu, to fight Kokushibo and Kanata waits for his response. Kiriya denies, stating that they will keep heading towards Muzan, confident that Sanemi, Gyomei, Muichiro, and Genya will emerge victorious. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 172 (Pages 4-5). [9]


Much later, Kuina informs Kiriya that a batch of Demon Slayer were arriving to Muzan's location before Kanata adds that a second batch was coming, much to his worry. Muzan then goes on a rampage slaughtering countless Demon Slayers, causing Kiriya to have a nervous breakdown. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 144. [10] Kanata witnesses Kuina slap their brother to prevent him from cracking from the pressure, and yell that the fight was not over yet. Kiriya composes himself and thanks his sisters, causing them both to cry. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 181 (Pages 1-5). [11]


Sunrise Countdown Arc

After Tanjiro, Giyu, Mitsuri, and Obanai begin to clash with Muzan, he realizes that Yushiro had taken control of Nakime and kills her in a panic, causing the Infinity Castle to collapse and rise the surface. Despite the Infinity Castle having been destroyed which forced Muzan to the surface, Kiriya is dismayed that they had emerged in a city and asks Kanata how long it would be until dawn. She replies that it would be one and a half hours, further unnerving him. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 184 (Page 2). [12]


Later on, when Nezuko Kamado is awoken by the spirit of her father warning her of Tanjiro's peril, she flees from the Butterfly Mansion. Kiriya initially orders for her to be chased down before immediately taking it back. Kanata is confused, stating that if Nezuko is captured by a demon they couldn't bring her back. Kiriya then tearfully explains that it was Kagaya's request that they leave Nezuko be. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 185 (Pages 4-6). [13]


During the fight against Muzan, the King of Demons was backed into a corner and unleashed a mighty shockwave so powerful that it killed Kasugai Crows, which even injured the Ubuyashiki siblings. When Kiriya asked about Tanjiro and Obanai's well-being, Kanata who was being aided by Tengen, responds that she couldn't see the battle anymore because the tailsman were displaced but dawn was a mere 25 minutes away. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 197 (Pages 6-7). [14] Sure enought, the sun rose, and in a final cowardly attempt of survival, Muzan swelled up his body into a giant, fleshy infant. Whilst the remaining Hashira and Demon Slayers did everything in their power to prevent Muzan from fleeing, Kanata prayed to her parents, sisters, and the gods to help them in their time of need. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 199. [15] Muzan eventually did succumb to the sunlight, and Kanata rushed to her brother's aid when he collapsed from exhaustion. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 200 (Pages 2-4). [16] Due to Yushiro's talisman burning in the sunlight, she was unaware about the brief struggle against a temporarily demonized Tanjiro.


Kiriya and his sisters would summon the only surviving Hashira, Giyu and Sanemi, for a final Hashira meeting; he officially disbanded the Demon Slayer Corps due to the demonic threat finally being annihilated. The siblings profoundly thank the Wind and Water Hashira for their efforts, before they exclaim that they don't need their gratitude as the Ubuyashiki family were the real reason the Corps survived as long as he did. Giyu praises Kiriya for his leadership and says that his father and ancestors would be very proud of him causing all three children to become overwhelmed with emotion and cry. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 204 (Pages 7-11). [17]


After Muzan's death, the Ubuyashiki curse would finally end with Kiriya, who realized this when he aged into his 20s and 30s. He spends his hours weeping and holding his wide, children, and surviving sisters.





Abilities

Extraordinary Memory: During the infiltration of the Infinity Castle by the Demon Slayer Corps, Kanata displays immense skill in memorization, having successfully memorized and mapped out the fortress's constantly changing structures. She also tracks the routes and locations of every Demon Slayer and Hashira within the fortress, providing logistical support to them along with her brother and sister.


Trivia

  • She is first named in Chapter 144.
    • However, her headpiece was initially obscured which made identification impossible until Chapter 147. Though mistakenly drawn on her left side, its shape confirms her identity to be the same as the Final Selection examiner since her only other surviving sister has a shorter headpiece.
  • She does not have good physical abilities. She is timid and faints when a spider touches the back of her hand. She is very good at the koto. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Volume 21, (Extra Pages). [18]
  • In Kimetsu Academy, Kanata was described as the 'glutton' of the Ubuyashiki quintuplets.
  • Since forcefully grabbing her hair, Genya Shinazugawa became meek and shy around girls; he apologized to Kanata sincerely, saying, "I'm sorry for hitting you", though she was not hurt by the incident and reassured him.
