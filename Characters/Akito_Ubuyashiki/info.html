Akito Ubuyashiki


Akito Ubuyashiki (産うぶ屋や敷しき 晟あき斗と Ubuyashiki Akito) is a minor character from Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, being exclusive to a 2021 art exhibition.

Appearance

As a child, Akito greatly resembled his posthumous nephew Kiriya, having a black bobcut adorned with a wisteria headpiece. He also wore a floaral print kimono with the exact same design as his nieces, and a striped obi with a circular design, supported with a obiage and obijime. Akito was seen with a gentle, if not reserved and passive expression.


Personality

Unlike the kind and understanding Kagaya or resentful and dour Senri, Akito appeared to be meek, quiet, and compliant. Due to his brother convincing him to set their home ablaze, Akito seemed to be unable to stand up for himself and carried out Senri's instructions without question, leading to his death.


History

Akito was the youngest chold of his generation of the Ubuyashiki family. His elder brother Senri, who resented his illness and his father's suicide as a result of his position as Oyakata, forced Akito to set fire to the mansion, as well as joining him to commit double-suicide. Their mother would die from the fire in an attempt to save them, leaving Kagaya as the sole survivor. Kimetsu no Yaiba Gotoge Koyoharu exhibition 2021 (via Crunchyroll) [1]
