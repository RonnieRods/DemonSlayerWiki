Relationships

Kagaya Ubuyashiki

As his only son, Kiriya was chosen to be Kagaya's successor at a young age. Like his sisters, Kiriya aided his sickly and blind father, from being a spokesperson during Final Selection to helping nurse him when he's at his most sickly. Despite the tragedy of losing his parents and older sisters, Kiriya took his new role as Kagaya's successor with grace and dignity. However, as time goes on, Kiriya slowly begins to crack under pressure, but with some help from Kuina slapping him, Kiriya takes into consideration that the burden he faces is identical to his father and forefathers', and composes himself.


When Nezuko unexpectedly flees from the Butterfly Mansion, Kiriya at first panics and decides to pursue, before feeling his father's spirit call out to him and ask that he leave Nezuko be. Despite the confusion, Kiriya, wanting to honor his deceased father, complies. When Muzan is finally killed, Giyu during the last Hashira meeting praises Kiriya's leadership and states that his father would be proud of him. Hearing this comment regarding his father, Kiriya begins to cry.



Kuina and Kanata Ubuyashiki

Kuina and Kanata are Kiriya's younger and youngest sisters respectively. It seemed like he was closest to Kanata, as they both acted as the announcers for Final Selection are seen alongside their father and mother on two seperate Hashira meetings. Kuina on the other hand interacts with him very little. However, that all changes when Kiriya assumes the role as Oyakata, when both sisters help Kiriya map the Infinity Castle. At first, the girls act as subordinates that take orders from their brother; from Kiriya telling Kanata to not cry and to continue mapping, to him telling them to not send Giyu and Tanjiro to fight Kokushibo. However, when Kiriya begins to have a nervous breakdown, Kuina snaps him out of it by slapping and reassuring him, although she held regret over it.


The girls continued helping Kiriya until they are victorious; all three siblings give their thanks to Giyu and Sanemi and cry as the former Water Hashira tells Kiriya that their father would be proud of them. Kiriya would stay close to his sisters even into adulthood, hugging them while crying when he realized that the curse was truly over.


Tanjiro Kamado

Kiriya attended the Final Selection that Tanjiro took and survived, congratulating him and the other passees. Although the two scarcely interact afterwards, Kiriya is shown to have a high estimation of him as a swordsman, trusting him and Giyu to head towards Muzan's direction. After the corps are disbanded, Kiriya gifts Tanjiro and a now-human Nezuko a box full of cash, although the siblings still chose to sell charcoal. Kiriya also reassured Tanjiro that Sanemi enjoyed his letters.


Yushiro

Yushiro used his Blood Demon Art to create talisman that Kiriya, Kuina, and Kanata used to map the Infinity Castle, and keep tabs on the Demon Slayers, up until the sunlight disintegrates them. Decades later in a modern day, an elderly Kiriya is Yushiro's pen pal, the ageless demon being his only surviving ally from the Taisho Era.
