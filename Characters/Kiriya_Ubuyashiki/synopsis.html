History

Kiriya was born to Kagaya Ubuyashiki and Amane Ubuyashiki as their third child and only son of five children. He was born after his two older quintuplet sisters and before his two younger quintuplet sisters. As the sole son of the family, he becomes the male heir, the next head of the Ubuyashiki family and the first in line to inherit his father's position as commander of the Demon Slayer Corps, something Kagaya mentioned during Sanemi Shinazugawa's first Hashira meeting to explain how his death would not matter. Kiriya also stood next to his father when he gave Sanemi Masachika Kumeno's will. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 168 (Pages 8-15). [1]


Synopsis

Final Selection Arc

Kiriya appears along with his sister, Kanata, at the Final Selection to greet and inform the Demon Slayers in-training of what their last trial will entail. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 6 (Pages 8-9). [2] After the test concludes, Kiriya describes the ranking system of the Demon Slayer Corps, Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 8 (Page 19). [3] keeps his composure when Genya Shinazugawa angrily assaults his sister, and asks the survivors to pick an ore for their Nichirin Sword. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 8 (Pages 24-25). [4]


Entertainment District Arc

Shortly after the battle in Yoshiwara ended with the deaths of Upper Rank demons Gyutaro and Daki, Kiriya's father Kagaya became ecstatic at the news. However, his celebration of the new turn of events was short-lived as his health began to deteriorate quickly, coughing blood as Amane rushed for her children to detch his medication. Kiriya was summoned to help Kagaya, worriedly calling to his father as he watched him coughing more and more.


Hashira Training Arc

Kiriya joined his mother and sister in leading the emergency Hashira meeting. He explained that the only swords of information regarding the marks on Muichiro Tokito and Mitsuri Kanroji were from a journal by a Demon Slayer who used the first Breathing Style. However, when Mitsuri attempted to explain how it manifested on her, it instead left Kiriya bewildered by her demonstration.


Sometime later, his parents devised a plan to set up an ambush for Muzan Kibutsuji, which involved blowing up their mansion with them in it. Kiriya's two elder sisters: Nichika and Hinaki chose to stay and die with their parents, while Kiriya was given a new haori resembling his fathers' and was set to assume the position of Oyakata. He, Kuina, and Kanata would be relocated to the hidden Butterfly Mansion, with retured Hashira Tengen Uzui and Shinjuro Rengoku serving as their bodyguards, a first time in generations for the family.


Infinity Castle Arc

After the death of Kiriya's parent and older sisters, Kiriya became the last leader of Demon Slayer Corps and commanded them on the battle of Infinity Castle. He officially stopped dressing like a girl and wore a talisman made by the friendly demon, Yushiro. He was assisted by his younger sisters Kuina and Kanata; all three mapped the Infinity Castle based on information delivered to them through the Kasugai Crows via the talisman, as well as received news on the events inside the castle, such as Shinobu's clash against Doma and Zenitsu confronting Kaigaku. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 144 (Pages 5-8). [5] Later on, his youngest sister Kanata would begin to tear up from the deaths of her parents and sisters, causing Kiriya to scold her and tell her to stop crying or stop moving her hand, to which she complied. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 147 (Page 1). [6]


During the battle against Upper Rank One Kokushibo, Sanemi remembered Kiriya's presence at his first Hashira meeting. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 168 (Pages 8-15). [1] In addition, a crow surveilled the battle and the siblings watched it via Yushiro's Blood Demon Art. Kuina asks Kiriya if they should send other slayers, specifically Tanjiro and Giyu, to fight Kokushibo. Kiriya denies, stating that they will keep heading towards Muzan, confident that Sanemi, Gyomei, Muichiro, and Genya will emerge victorious. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 172 (Pages 4-5). [7]


Although Kiriya is proven right, he begins to worry that Muzan would soon overpower Tamayo and recover from his injuries, before being told that two troops of Demon Slayers are reaching his location. Kiriya begs them to stop but he is too late; the Demon Slayers find Muzan's flesh cocoon and the King of Demons emerges from it and kills all present swordsmen, despite Kiriya's plea for them to wait for the Hashira.


Muzan then goes on a rampage while traversing the Infinity Castle, killing any Demon Slayer in his path despite Kiriya's desperate orders to fall back. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 180. [8] Kiriya then had a severe panic attack where he blamed himself for the casualties and fatalistically believed that the corps' efforts would be wasted.


His breakdown was interrupted by Kuina who slaps him across the face. After getting over the shock of her action, she then yells at Kiriya to get a hold of himself as the fight had not ended yet. Realizing that his father and ancestors have had the same burdens, Kiriya composes himself and orders the Demon Slayers to concentrate their manpower towards Muzan, before then thanks his sisters. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 181 (Pages 1-5). [9]


Sunrise Countdown Arc

After Tanjiro, Giyu, Mitsuri, and Obanai begin to clash with Muzan, he realizes that Yushiro had taken control of Nakime and kills her in a panic, causing the Infinity Castle to collapse and rise the surface. Despite the Infinity Castle having been destroyed which forced Muzan to the surface, Kiriya is dismayed that they had emerged in a city. He asks Kanata how long it would be until dawn, to which she replies that it would be one and a half hours, further unnerving Kiriya due to the time. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 184 (Page 2). [10]


After being awakened by the spirit of her father who told her that Tanjiro was in danger, Nezuko, who had also been inside the Butterfly Mansion fled to aid her brother. Shinjuro informs Kiriya of this and he begins to panic once again from the confusing turn of events. Deciding to chase after her, he gives the order to capture her before he feels the spirit of his own father who tells him to let her go. He then cancels his order much the confusion of his sisters, causing him to tearfully explain that it was Kagaya's request. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 185 (Pages 4-6). [11]


During the fight against Muzan, the King of Demons was backed into a corner and unleashed a mighty shockwave so powerful that it killed Kasugai Crows, which even injured the Ubuyashiki siblings. Despite bleeding from his nose, Kiriya insisted Shinjuro that he was fine and asked about Tanjiro and Obanai's well-being. Kanata responds that she couldn't see the battle anymore because the tailsman were displaced but dawn was a mere 25 minutes away. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 197 (Pages 6-7). [12] Sure enough, the sun rose, and in a final cowardly attempt of survival, Muzan swelled up his body into a giant, fleshy infant. Whilst the remaining Hashira and Demon Slayers did everything in their power to prevent Muzan fleeing, Kiriya desperately screamed to kill him and not let him escape and hatefully demanded Muzan to die. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 199. [13] Muzan eventually did succumb to the sunlight, leaving Kiriya exhausted and crying. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 200 (Pages 2-4). [14] Due to Yushiro's talisman burning in the sunlight, he was unaware about the brief struggle against a temporarily demonized Tanjiro.


Kiriya and his sisters would summon the only surviving Hashira, Giyu and Sanemi, for a final Hashira meeting. Explaining that despite the heavy casualties, the demonic threat was annihilated once and for all, and as such he officially disbanded the Demon Slayer Corps. The siblings thank the Wind and Water Hashira for their efforts, before they exclaim that they don't need their gratitude as the Ubuyashiki family were the real reason the Corps survived as long as he did. Giyu praises Kiriya for his leadership and says that his father and ancestors would be very proud of him. Hearing this, Kiriya thanked him and he and his sisters would begin to cry. Kimetsu no Yaiba Manga: Chapter 204 (Pages 7-11). [15]


Kiriya would later send Tanjiro and Nezuko a box full of money as a thanks for their efforts, although the siblings still chose to sell charcoal. Kiriya also took note of Tanjiro writing to Sanemi, and in response to him worrying that he was annoying the former Wind Hashira, Kiriya encouraged him to continue and said that he in fact enjoyed receiving letters. Kimetsu no Yaiba Second Fanbook. [16] Tanjiro's Status Report. [17]


Legacy

After Muzan's death, the Ubuyashiki curse would finally end with Kiriya, who realized this when he aged into his 20s and 30s. He spends his hours weeping and holding his wife, children, and surviving sisters.


Over a century after Muzan's defeat, Kiriya is still a alive and became the oldest living person in Japan. This achievement caused him to be interviewed by a reporter, although he remained unresponsive due to his elderly age. The fact he lived so long is more proof that the illness which previously cursed their family is now gone along along with Muzan. In addition, he's also pen pals with Yushiro, his only other ally to survive in the modern day.
