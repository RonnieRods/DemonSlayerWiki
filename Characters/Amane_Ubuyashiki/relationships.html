Relationships

Kagaya Ubuyashiki

Amane first met Kagaya when he was thirteen, having already taken his late father's position as Oyakata of the Demon Slayer Corps. Amane was selected to be his caretaker when his condition would deteriorate, but Kagaya only agreed to marry her under the condition that she cared for him under her own free will. Amane was loyal to Kagaya without a fault, caring for his every need as his illness worsened, and eventually taking over his position to speak at meetings with the Hashira when he was no longer able to do so. Later, when Kagaya decided to sacrifice himself to stall Muzan, Amane chose to stay with him until the very end and die by his side.


The Hashira

As with her husband, Amane was a highly respected figure to the Hashira. During the emergency meeting in the wake of the battle in the Swordsmith Village, Amane led the gathering in place of the bedridden Kagaya, informing them of the news while bowing. The Hashira became somber at the news but did not question Amane's authority and steadfastly supported her; Gyomei Himejima in particular offered her a prayer to remain strong in the face of hardships, which she was thankful for. Comedically, upon being asked to explain how she had awakened special marks in her battle, Mitsuri Kanroji became momentarily distracted due to admiring Amane. Additionally, Giyu Tomioka, who was struggling with a severe inferiority complex about his position as the Water Hashira, opted to leave the meeting instead of discussing future plans with his peers, did not depart until Amane had already left the meeting to let them discuss amongst themselves.


Muichiro Tokito

Amane was the one to recruit the Mist Hashira, Muichiro Tokito. Initially, she visited him three years in the past and informed him and his twin brother Yuichiro about the prospect of them becoming Demon Slayers. She also explained that they were descendants of powerful swordsman. Despite Muichiro's excitement at the idea of helping others, Yuichiro, who had become embittered by the deaths of their parents, rejected her offer and demanded she leave their home. This also indirectly led to a falling out between the twins as they clashed over their beliefs. Amane attempted to visit them several times afterwards but was driven away by Yuichiro each time.


After an attack by a demon left Yuichiro dead and Muichiro severely wounded and close to dying himself, Amane and her daughters, Nichika and Hinaki, found them in their home. Though Yuichiro had already begun to rot, Amane held Muichiro and saw he was still alive, praying for his survival as she ordered her daughters to give him immediate medical attention. When he woke up in the Ubuyashiki Mansion, Muichiro, filled with intense rage at the pain of his twin's death, began relentlessly training. Nichika was concerned and attempted to stop him but was halted by Amane, who despite knowing the fragile state his body was in, knew this was something he had to do.


Muzan Kibutsuji

Although not showing the same pure enmity as her husband, as the wife of the head of the Demon Slayer Corps, Amane was dedicated to ending the life of Muzan Kibutsuji. When Muzan intruded onto their mansion, Amane showed no fear and sat silently as her husband conversed with the progenitor of all demons. Because he was blind, Kagaya also asked Amane to describe his physical appearance to him.
