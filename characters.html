Quick Answers

Who are some of the demons featured in Demon Slayer?
The most well-known demons featured in Demon Slayer are Nezuko Kamado, Muzan Kibutsuji, Tamayo, and the members of the Twelve Kizuki. Other notable demons include Yushiro, the Swamp Demon, Yahaba, Susamaru, Kyogai, and the members of the Spider Family.
What are some of the unique abilities of the demons in Demon Slayer?
Demons exhibit prototypical powers such as enhanced strength and speed, heightened senses, and powerful regeneration. Some also display higher abilities known as Blood Demon Arts. These are unique to each individual and can range from powerful attacks and increased abilities, to manifesting illusions and changing one's innate physiology.
How does the Demon Slayer series differentiate between its various characters?
Demon Slayer distinguishes its characters between the Demon Slayer Corps and the demons. Within the Corps is the main unit of Demon Slayers, including Tanjiro Kamado, Zenitsu Agatsuma, and Inosuke Hashibira, the Hashira, and the Ubuyashiki Family. Within the demons is Muzan Kibutsuji, the members of the Twelve Kizuki, and solitary demons who are unaffiliated but still connected to Muzan.
